📊 GPTs for Data Analysis & BI

GPTs aren’t just for writing. They can help your team make sense of dashboards, spreadsheets, reports, and analytics tools — instantly and in plain English.

Why this matters

Data is only useful if your team understands and acts on it. GPTs act as natural-language translators for your KPIs — helping users spot patterns, flag issues, and get answers from complex reports.

Example Use Cases

  • Ask “Why did revenue dip last month?” — GPT explains the trend based on BI reports
  • Summarize SQL or Tableau dashboards in real-time
  • Convert spreadsheet columns into plain-text narratives
  • Automate insights and weekly reporting for non-technical teams

Tools it can connect with

GPTs can be embedded in tools like Notion, Google Sheets, Looker, or Airtable — or connected via API to analytics platforms and CRMs like Salesforce or HubSpot.

Who benefits

  • Sales & Marketing leads who want fast answers
  • Operations teams juggling spreadsheets & systems
  • Execs needing quick context, not dashboards
