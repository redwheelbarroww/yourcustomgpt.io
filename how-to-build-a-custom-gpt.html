🧩 How to Build a Custom GPT

You’ve probably used ChatGPT — but what if you could have one trained on your company’s content, answering exactly like your best rep? That’s the power of a custom GPT.

Step 1: Define the role

Is this GPT for support, internal ops, training, or lead capture? Great results start with a clear objective. A “Support GPT” is trained very differently than a “Product Explainer.”

Step 2: Gather source material

This might include help docs, pitch decks, sales emails, FAQs, PDFs, or internal SOPs. The quality of what you feed the model = the quality of what it can output.

Step 3: Train + prompt correctly

Custom instructions, formatting examples, tone guides, and “dos/don’ts” all shape how the GPT responds. This is where most DIY builds fall short — and where expert tuning makes a huge difference.

Step 4: Test and iterate

Before launch, we test it with dozens of questions to check consistency, tone, and edge cases. It’s like QA testing a new hire.

Step 5: Launch and monitor

Once live, we monitor usage, update based on feedback, and keep your GPT aligned with changing content or priorities.
