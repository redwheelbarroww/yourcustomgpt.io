🚀 How to Use a Custom GPT

A custom GPT is like an AI team member trained on your business materials. Here’s how you can put it to work for you — with zero technical expertise required.

1. Pick your use case

Whether you need help with customer support, internal training, content creation, or product FAQs, start with one key area. We'll train your GPT on materials specific to that job.

2. Provide your materials

This could include help docs, onboarding scripts, product descriptions, or blog posts. The richer the content, the smarter your GPT becomes.

3. Customize its voice and goals

Want your GPT to speak casually, professionally, or match your brand tone? We’ll prompt it accordingly — and give it clear objectives like “answer questions” or “summarize policies.”

4. Start using it!

You’ll receive a live link to test your GPT, share it with your team or customers, and even embed it into your site, Notion, Slack, or other tools.
