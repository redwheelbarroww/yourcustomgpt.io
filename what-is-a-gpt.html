What is a GPT?

A GPT (Generative Pre-trained Transformer) is an AI model designed to understand and generate human-like text. Businesses today are using custom GPTs as powerful assistants ‚Äî trained on their specific content and workflows to handle customer support, internal processes, and more.

What Does GPT Stand For?

GPT stands for "Generative Pre-trained Transformer." It means the AI has been trained on a broad set of information to understand language patterns, and can then generate intelligent, context-aware responses based on the data it's exposed to.

How Does a GPT Work?

A GPT uses deep learning algorithms to predict and generate natural-sounding responses. When customized for business use, a GPT can be fine-tuned on your company's documents, FAQs, service manuals, and more, allowing it to serve your customers or team with domain-specific knowledge.

Why Are Businesses Using GPTs?

Custom GPTs help automate tasks, reduce manual effort, improve customer experience, and open up new levels of personalization at scale. Whether it's handling customer inquiries or supporting internal training, GPTs create huge efficiencies for modern businesses.
